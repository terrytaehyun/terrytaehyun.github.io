Description

Designed for Daily Usage

Matamatics is a free scientific calculator that supports many common math operations such as trigonometric, exponential, and logarithmic operations.

Matamatics is ideal for users who need aids with high-school level mathematics.

Universal App

Matamatics is supported in iPod Touch, iPhone, and iPad devices with iOS version 7.1 or higher.

  • iPhone 4
  • iPhone 4S
  • iPhone 5
  • iPhone 5C
  • iPhone 5S
  • iPhone 6
  • iPhone 6 Plus
  • iPod Touch (5th Generation)
  • iPad Mini
  • iPad Mini 2
  • iPad Mini 3
  • iPad 2
  • iPad 3rd & 4th Generation
  • iPad Air

Bracket Friendly

Most calculator applications hide existing expression from the screen when bracket operator is in use. Often, use of embedded brackets are prohibited in these applications.

Matamatics evaluates input expressions as whole, and users are free to use as many brackets as they wish.
